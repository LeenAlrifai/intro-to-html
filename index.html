About Me

My Name is Leen Alrifai. I am 20 years old and Syrian. I was born in Amman Jordan and moved to Canada when I was only 1 years old and lived in Toronto for 13 years. My family and I eventally had to move out of Toronto and decided to live in Ottawa in 2017. In my free time I like to paint and watch shows. I am a fan of anime so most of my paintings I do are anime related. I am a huge car enthusiast and hope one day I will be able to learn how to modify/buy all types of vehicals as a side hustle. My favourite type of vehicals are JDMS, which stands for "Japanese Domestic Market." I own figures of some of my dream cars and I am currently building a mini Supra MK4 kit.

One of my passions is Photography, I love taking photos of anything I find Aesthetically pleasing. One of my favourite things to capture is the moon even though my Iphone 14 wont ever capture it's full potential, I still try everything I could to take a photo. Another passion of mine is editing shows and movies with my favourite songs. It is almost like thearpy and a good distraction or if I am feeling really bored, I just spend time creating edits.
